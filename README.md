# AI Impact on the Job Market — Tableau Dashboard (2024–2030)

> A data visualization portfolio project exploring how artificial intelligence is reshaping the global job market through 2030.

---

## About This Project

As AI continues to transform industries at an unprecedented pace, understanding its impact on employment is critical for workers, organizations, and policymakers alike.

This project uses **Tableau** to turn complex workforce data into clear, interactive dashboards that answer key questions:

- Which jobs are most at risk of automation?
- Where is job growth happening despite AI disruption?
- How are salaries shifting across AI-exposed roles?
- What does the future of work look like by 2030?

---

## Dashboards

| Dashboard | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Automation Risk by Role** | Visualizes which job categories face the highest risk of AI automation |
| **Job Growth Trends** | Highlights sectors growing despite — or because of — AI adoption |
| **Salary & Skills Analysis** | Explores how AI exposure correlates with compensation changes |
